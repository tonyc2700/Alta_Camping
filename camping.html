Laundry

We know how important it is to be able to clean your clothing after a long journey. Some of the features include:

  • 40mins to wash up to 8kg of clothing.
  • 40mins to dry up to 8kg of clothing.
  • Building is open 24/7.

Bathrooms

Toilets and showers are open 24/7. They are free to everyone camping here and we always make sure they are of the highest of hygiene standards.

Dining

The kitchen and dining area is open 24/7. We also have the outdoors sheltered BBQ pit and picnic tables, all of our campers and welcome to use them and we also sell food and extra materials you might need for a perfect dinner.

Shelter

Alta is blessed with good weather. The surrounding hills protect our campers from the wind and Alta is well known for being as dry as the Sahara Desert.

Mini Golf

Mini golf is a perfect way to bring the family together. We have many golf clubs, golf balls and score-sheets for you to have some fun.

Kids Park

We have a park near the barbecue pit, all kids are welcome to play on the park. We have small bicycles and tricycles for the kids to play with too.

WiFi

Internet is free to use for all guests. We have an extra strong wireless transmitter so you can have goot access to the internet anywhere within the grounds of Alta Strand.

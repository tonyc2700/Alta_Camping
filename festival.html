History

The Alta winter festival is hosted around the Finnmark annual dog sled competition. 1981 the first dog sled competition began and ended in the Alta Strand Camping grounds. Since then, the race has grown so much that a whole festival is hosted around it.

Festival

The festival lasts 5 days and includes:

  • Exhibitions
  • Talks
  • Presentation
  • Cinema
  • And more!

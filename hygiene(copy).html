Laundry

We know how important it is to be able to clean your clothing after a long journey. Some of the features include:

  • 40mins to wash up to 8kg of clothing.
  • 40mins to dry up to 8kg of clothing.
  • Building is open 24/7.

Showers

Just as we know that it's important to wash your clothing, good showers are a haven when camping. Some of the features include:

  • Free showers to all guests.
  • 2 buildings.
  • Buildings are open 24/7.

Toilets

Good toilets are a must. Some of the features include:

  • Free use of toilets to all guests.
  • 2 buildings.
  • Buildings are open 24/7.

Sauna

Why not relax in steamy sauna with a terrace and a river view.

  • Holds up to 5 people.
  • Booking is required 1 hour prior to use.

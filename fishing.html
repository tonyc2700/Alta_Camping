River

Alta has a strong history with salmon fishing in the Alta River. Licenses to fish here are very rare and hard to get, for more information please visit http://www.altalaks.no/Default.aspx?id=11

Sea

As well as the river, the sea has plenty to offer. Fish can be caught all over Alta, everywhere from the shore to fishing from a boat.

Lakes

There are lakes all over the hills around Alta, they are abundant with many types of trout. A short 20 minute hike is all you need to catch a tasty trout.
